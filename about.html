Who are we

We are experts
in logistics

We are a company dedicated to the comprehensive management of cargo, executing various logistical processes, from an origin abroad to Colombia. We are an organization that combines youth with a high level of experience, bringing fresh and innovative ideas, and we have been in the market for over 15 years.

Our goal as an organization is to represent all those companies and investors whose commercial activities require specialized services in international markets. In this way, we aim to establish ourselves as a strategic ally, providing commercial, logistical, and legal support that allows our clients to confidently and securely finalize their business transactions.

Mision

SKYLNE SHIPMENT is an organization dedicated to the importation, marketing, and distribution of goods and services. Additionally, we provide solutions in international logistics for individuals and legal entities that need to carry out foreign trade operations.

Vision

SKYLNE SHIPMENT will be recognized in the main cities of Colombia as one of the most reliable and secure companies in international logistics solutions and foreign trade processes. We will create new business units and strategic alliances in maritime ports and managed free trade zones.
